About PhilTravel

Founded in 2016, PhilTravel has been dedicated to creating unforgettable Philippine experiences for travelers worldwide. We combine passion for this beautiful country with professional expertise to deliver exceptional tours.

Our mission is simple: to make Philippine tourism accessible, affordable, and absolutely magical for everyone who visits.

With a team of experienced guides and local experts, we ensure every journey is safe, comfortable, and filled with authentic cultural experiences.

Our Mission

To provide world-class, sustainable travel experiences that showcase the natural beauty, rich culture, and warm hospitality of the Philippines while supporting local communities and conservation efforts.

Our Vision

To be the leading Philippine travel agency recognized for innovation, sustainability, and delivering life-changing adventures that create lasting memories and foster genuine connections.
